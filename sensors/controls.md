# Controls & states

Once a camera has [sensors set up](/sensors/setup), you can see their state and operate the ones that support it.

## Where sensors appear

- **The camera's Overview** (in the [settings](/cameras/settings) drawer) lists every sensor with its live state and, for controllable ones, the controls.
- **[Shortcuts](/cameras/shortcuts)** let you place a sensor on the video to read its state on hover and operate it on click.

## Read-only sensors

These show their current state but aren't operated:

- **Contact.** Open or closed, for a door or window.
- **Temperature** and **humidity.** The current reading.
- **Occupancy.** Occupied or empty.
- **Smoke** and **leak.** Clear or triggered.
- **Battery.** Level and charging.

## Controllable accessories

<Shot src="/img/sensors/accessory-controls.png" alt="Accessory controls in the camera overview" />

These you can operate (admin only):

- **Light.** On or off, and brightness where supported.
- **Switch.** On or off.
- **Lock.** Lock or unlock.
- **Siren.** On or off, and volume where supported.
- **Garage.** Open or close.
- **Doorbell.** Trigger a ring.
- **Security system.** Choose a state: disarmed, stay arm, away arm, or night arm.

PTZ cameras have their own [controls and autotrack](/sensors/ptz).
