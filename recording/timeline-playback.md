# Timeline & playback

Open a camera and use its **timeline** to scrub through recorded footage. Drag along the timeline to move through time; marks show where events happened.

![Recording timeline and scrubber](/img/recording/timeline.png)

## Reviewing footage

- **Scrub** smoothly along the timeline. camera.ui uses the low-resolution stream, so scrubbing stays fast.
- **Play, pause, rewind, and fast-forward**, and change the **speed** from slow to several times faster.
- **Hover** over an event to preview it before jumping in.
- **Jump to a day** with the calendar, which highlights the days that have footage.

## Several cameras at once

In [Camview](/cameras/camview), the timeline scrubs **all cameras in the layout together**, keeping them in sync. This is ideal for following something across cameras at the same moment in time.

## Trimming

Drag the trim handles on the timeline to select a range, then save it. See [Export & download](/recording/export).
