# Export & download

You can save part of a recording as a file to keep or share.

## Export a clip

![Trimming a clip on the timeline](/img/recording/export-trim.png)

On the [timeline](/recording/timeline-playback), drag the trim handles to select the start and end of the part you want, then export. camera.ui produces an **MP4** file and gives you a download.

## Timelapse

You can also export a **timelapse**, which compresses a long period into a short clip by sampling frames at an interval. Good for reviewing a whole day quickly.
